Our Standards

Excellence in Every Detail

Our team upholds the highest standards across every aspect of the dining experience — from how we look to how we serve.

👔

Professional Image

Our team presents a polished, culturally appropriate appearance at all times. Uniform standards, dress and grooming guidelines for both Wahine and Kāne are outlined to ensure a consistent and respectful representation of the PCC brand.

🤝

Work Etiquette

We foster a respectful, collaborative, and professional work environment. Our etiquette guidelines support positive relationships with guests and teammates alike, reflecting the Aloha spirit in everything we do.

🛡️

Safety & Sanitation

The health and safety of our guests and team is our top priority. We follow strict food safety, sanitation, and kitchen hygiene protocols to maintain the highest level of quality and care in every service.

📅

Attendance & Scheduling

Dependability is a cornerstone of our team. We use UKG workforce management tools and clear scheduling policies to ensure every shift is covered and our guests experience seamless, uninterrupted service.

📋

Policies & Procedures

Clear, documented procedures guide every operational role — from setup to service to closing. Our SOPs ensure consistency, accountability, and a high-quality experience for every guest who dines with us.

🌱

Growth & Development

We invest in the professional growth of every team member. Through hands-on training, mentorship, and leadership opportunities, our department empowers employees to build skills that last a lifetime.
